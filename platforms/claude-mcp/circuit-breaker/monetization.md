# Circuit Breaker - Monetization Strategy

## Pricing Tiers

### Open Source (Free)
- Basic circuit breaker
- Local state only
- Up to 10 circuits
- Community support

### Pro ($19/month)
- Distributed state (Redis)
- Unlimited circuits
- Fallback handlers
- Email support

### Business ($79/month)
- Advanced analytics
- Custom thresholds
- Prometheus integration
- Priority support

### Enterprise (Custom)
- Multi-region support
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantees
- Professional services
