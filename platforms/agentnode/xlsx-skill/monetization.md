## Risk Factors

### Market Risks
1. **Microsoft Dominance**: Excel has 750M+ users
   - Mitigation: API-first approach, automation focus

2. **Google Sheets Competition**: Free alternative
   - Mitigation: Advanced features, enterprise focus

3. **Desktop Excel**: Offline capabilities
   - Mitigation: Cloud-native advantages, API automation

### Technical Risks
1. **Formula Complexity**: Edge cases in calculations
   - Mitigation: Comprehensive testing, validation tools

2. **Performance at Scale**: Large spreadsheet handling
   - Mitigation: Streaming processing, pagination
