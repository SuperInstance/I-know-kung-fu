## Risk Factors

### Market Risks
1. **Cloud Provider Competition**: Google/Microsoft could offer free alternatives
   - Mitigation: Focus on superior API and developer experience

2. **Price Erosion**: Commoditization of document processing
   - Mitigation: Add value through AI features, templates

3. **Enterprise Sales Cycles**: Long sales cycles for enterprise deals
   - Mitigation: Self-serve onboarding, proof-of-concept offerings

### Technical Risks
1. **Scalability Challenges**: High-volume periods
   - Mitigation: Auto-scaling infrastructure, queue management

2. **Data Security Concerns**: Enterprise security requirements
   - Mitigation: SOC 2, HIPAA compliance, transparent policies
