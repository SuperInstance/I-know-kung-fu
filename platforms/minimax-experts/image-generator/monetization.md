## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

#### Free Tier
- 20 images per month
- 512x512 resolution only
- Standard quality
- Community support
- **Purpose**: User acquisition

#### Starter Tier - $9.99/month
- 200 images per month
- Up to 1024x1024 resolution
- High quality
- Email support
- Basic styles
- **Target**: Individual creators

#### Professional Tier - $29.99/month
- 1,000 images per month
- Up to 2048x2048 resolution
- Ultra quality
- Priority support
- All styles unlocked
- Background removal
- API access
- **Target**: Professional creators, marketers

#### Business Tier - $79.99/month
- 5,000 images per month
- Unlimited resolution
- Team collaboration
- Brand kit support
- Dedicated support
- Custom model training
- **Target**: Agencies, teams

#### Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing
- Unlimited images
- Custom model fine-tuning
- On-premise option
- SLA guarantees
- **Target**: Large enterprises
