# Document Processor Monetization Strategy

## Market Analysis

### Target Market Segments

**Primary Segments:**
1. **Legal Professionals** - Law firms, corporate legal departments, legal aid organizations
2. **Enterprise Business** - Fortune 500 companies, mid-market businesses
3. **Academic Institutions** - Universities, research organizations, students
4. **Government Agencies** - Federal, state, and local government offices

**Secondary Segments:**
- Marketing agencies
- HR departments
- Consulting firms
- Healthcare organizations

### Market Size
- Global document processing market: $8.5 billion (2024)
- Expected CAGR: 12.4% through 2028
- Word processing software users: 1.2 billion globally

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

#### Free Tier
- 5 document operations per month
- Basic formatting only
- Community support
- **Purpose**: User acquisition and trial conversion

#### Professional Tier - $29.99/month
- 100 document operations per month
- All formatting features
- Tracked changes support
- Email support
- Document templates library
- **Target**: Individual professionals, freelancers

#### Business Tier - $99.99/month
- Unlimited document operations
- Team collaboration features
- Priority support
- Custom templates
- API access
- Usage analytics
- **Target**: Small to medium businesses

#### Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing
- Volume licensing
- On-premise deployment option
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantees
- **Target**: Large organizations
