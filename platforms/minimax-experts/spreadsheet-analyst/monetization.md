## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

#### Free Tier
- 10 operations per month
- Basic analysis only
- 10,000 row limit
- Community support
- **Purpose**: User acquisition

#### Starter Tier - $19.99/month
- 100 operations per month
- All basic features
- 100,000 row limit
- Email support
- Formula generation
- **Target**: Individual analysts, students

#### Professional Tier - $49.99/month
- 500 operations per month
- Advanced analysis features
- 1,000,000 row limit
- Priority support
- Custom templates
- API access
- **Target**: Professional analysts, consultants

#### Business Tier - $149.99/month
- Unlimited operations
- All features unlocked
- Team collaboration
- Shared templates
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- **Target**: Teams, departments

#### Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing
- Volume licensing
- On-premise option
- Custom development
- SLA guarantees
- Training included
- **Target**: Large enterprises
