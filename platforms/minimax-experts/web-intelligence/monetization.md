4. **Media & Publishing** - Journalists, content creators, editorial teams

**Secondary Segments:**

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

#### Free Tier
- 20 searches per month
- 5 extractions per month
- Basic results only
- Community support
- **Purpose**: User acquisition and evaluation

#### Starter Tier - $14.99/month
- 200 searches per month
- 50 extractions per month
- Standard synthesis
- Email support
- Basic monitoring
- **Target**: Individual researchers, students

#### Professional Tier - $49.99/month
- 1,000 searches per month
- 250 extractions per month
- Advanced synthesis with citations
- Priority support
- Full monitoring capabilities
- API access
- **Target**: Professional researchers, consultants

#### Business Tier - $149.99/month
- Unlimited searches
- Unlimited extractions
- Team collaboration features
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
- Advanced monitoring
- **Target**: Teams, departments

#### Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing
- Volume licensing
- Custom data sources
- White-label options
- SLA guarantees
- Dedicated infrastructure
- **Target**: Large enterprises
