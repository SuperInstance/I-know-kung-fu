# Presentation Builder Monetization Strategy

## Market Analysis

### Target Market Segments

**Primary Segments:**
1. **Sales Teams** - Enterprise sales, B2B sales, account executives creating pitch decks
2. **Marketing Departments** - Brand managers, content marketers, campaign managers
3. **Executives** - C-suite, directors, managers presenting to boards and stakeholders
4. **Consultants** - Management consultants, strategy consultants, freelance advisors

**Secondary Segments:**
- Training and development professionals
- Academic presenters
- Startup founders
- Event speakers

### Market Size
- Global presentation software market: $4.8 billion (2024)
- Expected CAGR: 9.2% through 2028
- PowerPoint users: 500 million worldwide
- Presentations created daily: 30 million+

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Pricing Model

#### Free Tier
- 5 presentations per month
- Basic templates only
- 10 slides maximum
- Community support
- **Purpose**: User acquisition and trial

#### Starter Tier - $14.99/month
- 20 presentations per month
- All templates included
- 30 slides maximum
- Email support
- Speaker notes generation
- **Target**: Individual professionals

#### Professional Tier - $34.99/month
- Unlimited presentations
- Custom templates and themes
- Unlimited slides
- Priority support
- Chart generation from data
- PDF export included
- **Target**: Business professionals

#### Business Tier - $99.99/month
- Team collaboration features
- Brand kit management
- Template library sharing
- Analytics and tracking
- API access
- Dedicated support
- **Target**: Teams and departments

#### Enterprise Tier - Custom Pricing
- Volume licensing
- On-premise option
- Custom integrations
- Training and onboarding
- SLA guarantees
- **Target**: Large enterprises
