# MiniMax Experts Platform

> **Platform Overview and Strategy**

## About MiniMax

MiniMax is a leading Chinese AI company that has launched an Experts marketplace where developers can create and monetize AI experts. Users pay credits to use experts, and creators earn based on usage.

### Key Stats (2026)
- Revenue grew from $10M (Sept 2024) to $70M (Sept 2025) - 7x increase
- IPO'd in Hong Kong raising HK$4.82 billion
- Strong growth in international markets (70% of revenue from overseas)

## How MiniMax Experts Work

1. **Create an Expert** - Build an AI agent with specific capabilities
2. **Submit for Review** - MiniMax reviews and approves
3. **List on Marketplace** - Users discover your expert
4. **Earn Credits** - Get paid when users invoke your expert

### Credit System
- Users purchase credits to use experts
- Experts have different credit costs based on complexity
- Creators earn a share of credits spent on their experts

## Best Practices

1. **Niche Focus**: Specific experts outperform general ones
2. **Clear Descriptions**: Users need to understand value quickly
3. **Quality Testing**: Errors lead to poor reviews
4. **Regular Updates**: Keep experts current
5. **User Feedback**: Iterate based on reviews
